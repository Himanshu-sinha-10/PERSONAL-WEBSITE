Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Best inspiration for students

Dr. Kalam, Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India’s missile and nuclear weapons programs

Here's a time line of Dr. Kalam's life:

  • 1931 - Born in Rameshwaram, India.
  • 1960 - Graduation from Madaras Institute Of Technology.
  • 1969 - Got the Government’s approval to expand the programme by including more engineers and scientists.
  • 1980 - SLVIII successfully launched satellite Rohini to orbit on July
  • 1981 - Padma Bhushan.
  • 1992 - He was the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and the Secretary of Defence Research and Development Organisation till December 1999.
  • 1997 - Awarded Bharat Ratna.
  • 2002 - Became 11th President of India.
  • 2015 - Died at the age of 83.
